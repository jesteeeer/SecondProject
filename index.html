BATANES

HOME OF THE WINDS

VISIT NOW

Batanes, known as the “Home of the Winds,” lies at the northernmost part of the Philippines. It is a small group of islands blessed with rolling green hills, rocky cliffs, and endless views of the sea. The islands are peaceful and untouched, offering a quiet escape from the busy life of the city.

The people of Batanes, called Ivatans, are known for their kindness, honesty, and simple way of living. They build strong stone houses that can withstand strong winds and typhoons. Farming and fishing are part of their daily life, showing their close connection to nature.

THE ISLANDS OF BATANES

Batanes is made up of ten small islands, but only three are inhabited — Batan, Sabtang, and Itbayat. Each island has its own unique beauty and story to tell.

BATAN ISLAND

The center of activity in Batanes where the capital, Basco, is located. It's home to beautiful rolling hills, lighthouses, and most tourist spots.

SABTANG ISLAND

Known for its traditional Ivatan stone houses and quiet villages that preserve the old way of life, offering visitors a peaceful and authentic experience.

ITBAYAT ISLAND

The northernmost and largest island, known for its rocky cliffs and adventurous boat ride. It's a great place for those seeking untouched natural beauty.

PLACES TO VISIT

Explore the most beautiful sights of Batanes, where nature and tradition come together. Each spot invites you to slow down, take in the view, and feel the calm of the “Home of the Winds.”

PLACES TO VISIT

BASCO LIGHTHOUSE

Standing proudly on Naidi Hills, the Basco Lighthouse offers a stunning view of the sea, town, and rolling hills. It's one of the best spots to watch the sunrise or sunset in Batanes.

MARLBORO HILLS

Known for its wide open fields and grazing cows, Marlboro Hills shows the peaceful beauty of Batanes. The green hills and ocean view create a feeling of freedom and calm.

VALUGAN BOULDER BEACH

Instead of sand, this beach is covered with smooth volcanic rocks formed by ancient eruptions. The strong waves and sound of the sea make it a perfect place to relax and reflect.

HONESTY COFFEE SHOP

A small unmanned store in Ivana where no one watches over the items. Visitors simply pay honestly, showing the true spirit and values of the Ivatan people.

TUKON CHURCH (MT. CARMEL CHAPEL)

Built on top of a hill, Tukon Church offers a stunning view of the surrounding mountains and sea. Its stone design blends beautifully with the natural scenery of Batanes.

HOUSE OF DAKAY (STONE HOUSE)

Located in Uyugan, this spot marks the famous “end of the road” in Batanes. The cliffs and rock formations make it a favorite place for photos and sightseeing.
